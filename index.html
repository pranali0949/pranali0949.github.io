Motion Graphics Portfolio

A selection of motion graphics, animation, and visual storytelling projects.

Texture | 3D Animation

Description: A texture-focused 3D animation study exploring material surfaces, lighting interaction, and motion within a minimal digital environment. Tools Used: Blender Design Goal: To demonstrate understanding of material rendering, texture detailing, lighting behavior, and object animation in 3D space.

3D Motion | Motion Graphics

Description: A cinematic 3D motion graphics piece exploring atmosphere, composition, and environmental storytelling through smooth camera movement and immersive visual pacing. Tools Used: Blender, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro Design Goal: To create a visually calming and cinematic experience while demonstrating skills in 3D composition, lighting, motion flow, and scene rendering.

Beer Advertisement | Motion Graphics

Description: A short-form commercial animation designed to highlight product visibility using bold typography, vibrant color contrast, and dynamic product motion. Tools Used: Adobe After Effects, Blender, Adobe Illustrator Design Goal: To produce an engaging promotional advertisement focused on product presentation, motion timing, and modern commercial visual design.

Astronot

Description: A conceptual motion graphics project combining minimal environments and character animation to create a futuristic visual sequence. Tools Used: Blender, Adobe After Effects Design Goal: To experiment with cinematic framing, movement, and visual storytelling within a science-fiction inspired motion design concept.

Twilling Garden

Description: An immersive digital installation project developed for a multimedia exhibition environment, featuring animated visual elements and atmospheric composition. Tools Used: Adobe After Effects, Blender, Adobe Photoshop Design Goal: To create an engaging visual storytelling experience that blends motion graphics and environmental design for exhibition-based presentation.

Pyro Fluidink | 3D Animation

Description: A fluid and particle simulation project experimenting with abstract volumetric motion and dynamic color interactions. Tools Used: Blender, Adobe After Effects Design Goal: To explore procedural simulation techniques and create visually expressive motion through fluid dynamics and particle-based animation.

Partical Dissolve | 3D Animation

Description: A particle-based animation sequence featuring organic dissolve effects and abstract motion transitions using layered simulation techniques. Tools Used: Blender, Adobe After Effects Design Goal: To develop advanced particle animation skills while creating visually dynamic transition effects and atmospheric digital compositions.
